About me

Graduate of The Iron Yard 12 week intensive coding bootcamp. With a focus on back end concepts and the Java language specifically. Born in Pittsburgh, PA in 1992 my family and I moved to Beaufort, SC around 2002. Went to College of Charleston in 2010 and stayed in Charleston since. I like to travel around when possible and see new places.

I enjoy solving new problems and even thinking of different ways to solve existing problems. One of the most interesting things about developing is how many different ways you can solve the same problem. I particularly find interacting with databases to be one of the most interesting things in programming and love learning more about it all.
